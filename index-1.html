📖 Our Story

Started with
₹5,000
& a Mission

In 2023, two brothers from Nagpur — Pranil and Rohit Mankar — saw a problem that no one was solving: students in tier-2 and tier-3 cities were making life-changing decisions about their education with zero proper guidance.

They launched EduOnTech with just ₹5,000, a shared belief in honest advice, and a mission to give every student access to the kind of guidance that was only available to the privileged few. Today, they've helped 100+ students find the right path — and they're just getting started.
